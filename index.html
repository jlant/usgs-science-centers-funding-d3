Interactive USGS Science Centers Funding Map and Bar Charts

By Jeremiah Lant

Description

Interactive map and charts of total funding for USGS Water Science Centers by state during the year 2015. The data is provided by the United States Geological Survey (USGS). The choropleth map shows total funding by state (for the data available). The bar charts show the funding by state broken down into component parts of appropriated funds, reimbursable funds, and matching funds.
